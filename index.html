HOW TO PLAY

BUY

BUY THE OFFICE

Buy desks to create seats, and computers so your hires don't work at half speed. No desk, no hire.

HIRE

HIRE WEIRDOS

Every candidate has a personality: 10x Engineers combust, Crypto Bros launch rogue tokens, Zen Masters never burn out. Salaries drain cash every second.

MAKE

INVEST & SHIP

Buy your first server, then pick an idea and set its AMBITION: bigger projects cost more up front but are far more likely to land at launch. A flop ships anyway - it just barely earns.

LIVE

LAUNCH = THE BIG BANG

Stealth is calm but not free: no VCs, no chaos, and cash steadily bleeds. Your first launch wakes the world - burn jumps, the press circles, disasters begin and funding opens.

VC

PITCH IN THE VC ROOM

Once you're live, VCs appear. Combine buzzwords and meet each round's product/hype bar to close Pre-Seed -> Series C. Close Series C to win.

FIRE

SURVIVE THE CHAOS

Once live: server fires cost cash if ignored - click them. PR disasters drain Hype; Pivot clears them for a fee. Caffeinate the team before burnout.

CASH

Your bank account. Servers and product funding spend it up front; live products refill it. Stealth bleeds steadily and burn jumps once you launch. Bottoms out = Game Over.

HYPE

Multiplies live revenue and unlocks funding rounds. Dormant in stealth - once you launch it decays fast, so feed it.
